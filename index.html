About the Institution

Thiagarajar College of Engineering(TCE), Madurai, an ISO 9001:2008 certified Institution affiliated to Anna University is one among the several educational and philanthropic institutions founded by Philanthropist and Industrialist Late. Shri. Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar. It was established in the year 1957. TCE is funded by Central & State Governments and Management. The courses offered in TCE are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. It was granted Autonomy in the year 1987. TCE has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. TCE offers Eight Undergraduate Programmes, Thirteen Postgraduate Programmes and Doctoral Programmes in Engineering, Science and Architecture.

About QUEST 2K16

Quest is an enthralling cultural frolic and an inter-school extravaganza which unites students from pre-school to higher secondary, where they can ignite their minds, abilities, artistic sense and provide a path for “change” in the society.

Quest was proposed in the year 1997 to mark the 50th year of our independence. Quest prolongs to augment values and cognizance to the youth through various themes such as

“Patriotism, Environmental awareness, Vision 2020, Water and Energy Conservation, Innovation , Smart Madurai, Village: yesterday, today, tomorrow, Clean India...”

The program consists of several events categorized as cultural events, literary events, skill events, Special events and Fun events based on main theme. Every year this program is organized and conducted by 3rd year students of our college under the guidance of staff members of our College Cultural Association and Final year students.

Quest 2016 encompasses itself on the theme “CELEBRATING INDIA” and it is to be held on 6th of February. The Quest focuses on the words of Mark Twain “India is the cradle of the human race, the birthplace of human speech, the mother of history, the grandmother of legend and the great grandmother of tradition”

It invites all young and fresh minds to come with innovative ideas. This event is mainly conducted for the welfare of students and to make them realize who they are, and what their potential is. We assure that the day will be a stepping stone for all inquisitive young minds to achieve greater heights.

Under the able guidance and benevolence of the Chairman and Correspondent Thiru Karumuttu.T.Kannan and spirited leadership of the Principal Dr.V.Abhai Kumar, Quest has been celebrated for 19 years and now it is stepping into its grand 20th year.

TCE, where quality and ethics matters is a GATEWAY for aspiring students to excel in their academics and a platform to explode their creativity, talents etc., QUEST 2K16 provides a platform for sharing their ideas for “CELEBRATING INDIA”.
